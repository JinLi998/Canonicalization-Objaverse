Part Manual Annotation and Evaluation

To validate the quality of our method's canonicalization, we manually annotated 487 categories, comprising approximately 1.9w objects. Among them, 81% were successfully canonicalized, 9% were unsuccessfully canonicalized, and 10% were noise data.

Comparison with the Previous Canonicalization Dataset

The previous canonicalization dataset was manually canonicalized, with each object taking about one minute, which limits the normalization of large-scale 3D data. Our automated canonicalization method addresses this challenge. We completed the canonicalization of the Objaverse-LIVS dataset in one week using six A40 GPUs. In the future, we plan to further process approximately 8w data points from the Objaverse-1.0 dataset.
